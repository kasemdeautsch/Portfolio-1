Syrian Customs and Traditions

Syria has a rich cultural heritage with diverse customs and traditions influenced by its long history and various ethnic groups.

Hospitality

Syrians are known for their warm hospitality. Guests are often greeted with coffee or tea and treated with great respect[1].

Traditional Clothing

Traditional Syrian clothing varies by region but often includes long, loose-fitting garments. Women may wear hijabs or other head coverings[1].

Cuisine

Syrian cuisine is diverse and flavorful, featuring dishes like kibbeh, shawarma, and baklava. Meals are often social events shared with family and friends[1].
